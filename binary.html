How Does a Binary MLM Works?

In a binary MLM, members earn commissions by building a team of distributors and selling products. Each member is placed at the top of a binary tree, with two branches beneath them. As new members are recruited, these branches can continue to grow. Members earn commissions on the sales made by their team, as well as a percentage of the sales made by the members in their downline. This allows members to earn residual income through the efforts of their team.
